MEDIFIX HEALTHCARE
.

Over 4 years experience in industry

We have started our journey in 2014 with a view to making inspired contributions to promising pharmaceutical sector in Sri Lanka and abroad.

We started as a pharmaceutical trading company engaging in to a strategic partnership with the trusted local manufacturing company Rine Pharmaceuticals and now we are a fastest growing innovative pharmaceutical company in Sri Lanka for nearly last two years.

Currently we are marketing and distributing products under our own brand names developed complying with the international standard and there are 13 new products in the process of testing and registration.

Overview Our Vision & Mision

Our Vision

To be the inspired Sri Lankan healthcare solution provider

Our Mission

To create a lifelong solution for the healthier life, empower joyful people and uplift the community through inspired healthcare

Strategies – Corporate Functions
.

The total pharmaceutical market of Sri Lanka is approximately Rs 27 Billion (US$ 240 million) of which the private retail market accounts for approximately 60% of sales while the government hospital purchases account for approximately 28%, private hospitals account for approximately 10% and dispensing family physi- cians account for approximately 2% of the total pharmaceutical business..

Medifix Health Care is well-furnished to reach with their marketing campaign to all the key consultants, doctors, government and Privet hospitals and family physicians. All our marketing campaigns are different and unique, so the clients can be sure that we do everything in our capability to manage a productive campaign in their best interest.

The Marketing team of 10 Medical Representatives is guided and managed by 02 Regional Managers and the Director of Marketing.

In order to cater to the emerging hyper market and key accounts, we have a well experienced manager to serve these institutions exclusively.

Existing distribution network is expanded island wide covering all major Towns and Cities which includes northern and eastern provinces. As our distribution strategy,

We have divided the whole island in to 10 main subdivisions and have appointed one each of the most efficient distribution network for respective areas.

There are 10 distribution deports in the island wide which covers every single chemist includes rural area in northern and eastern provinces as well.

The company’s own distribution network covers approximately 100 institutes and major chemists mainly in Colombo and other major cities.

The strategy to cover relatively small geographical area enables each distributor to cater every major town by 2-3 times per month.

We understand the needs of our customers and delivers value beyond expecta- tions. As well as it emphasizes the importance of all employees showing a ‘Passion for Brilliance.’ Customer satisfaction has always been the key to Medifix Health Care success. It is parallel important that it ‘live’ this values when dealing with all internal and external customers.

What we do today

To perform Market analysis in healthcare industry for our manufactures and suppliers.

To obtain Registrations to manufactures and suppliers from Sri Lankan government authority.
