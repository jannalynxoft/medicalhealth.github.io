Welcome to Medifix Healthcare

Best Residential & Commercial Wood Services

We have started our journey in 2014 with a view to making inspired contributions to promising pharmaceutical sector in Sri Lanka and abroad. We started as a pharmaceutical trading company engaging in to a strategic partnership with the trusted local manufacturing company Rine Pharmaceuticals and now we are a fastest growing innovative pharmaceutical company in Sri Lanka for nearly last two years.

Currently we are marketing and distributing products under our own brand names developed complying with the international standard and there are 13 new products in the process of testing and registration.

Medifix Health Care is in a comfortable position to compete against other competitive international products in Sri Lankan Market in terms of quality and price. Our quality level is assured to be better than many of the products from developing manufacturing companies as we have to meet stringent quality requirements of National Medical Regulatory Authority of Sri Lanka.

A team of highly skilled and dedicated professionals are responsible for effective- ly carrying out the tasks of sales promotion and distribution of our inventive

brands to the competitive and dynamic market. We have nurtured a trusted relationship with our most valued stakeholders in the industry and aspire to deliver only the finest pharmaceutical solutions to each and every one of them.

Medifix Health Care has done well within a relatively short time-span to be within the trusted pharmaceutical distributors in this country and has been able to improve turnover substantially from year-to-year and has achieved a strong standing in the marketplace.
